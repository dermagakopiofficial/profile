Our Story

The Pier of Tranquility

In the middle of the city's hustle and bustle, we built a dock. **Dermaga Kopi** (Coffee Pier) is designed as a sanctuary where time slows down. Inspired by the raw aesthetic of industrial ports and the calm of the sea at night, our space offers more than just caffeine.

Whether you are starting a voyage or looking for a place to dock after a long day, our baristas are ready to serve you the perfect anchor.
